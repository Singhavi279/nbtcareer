What is the cost of attending?

The registration fee is INR 399, which secures your dedicated 15-minute 1-on-1 counselling session in a private cabin as well as access to the college exhibition booths.

Absolutely. Career decisions are family decisions. We highly encourage parents to attend the mainstage sessions and participate in the guidance process.

We recommend setting aside 2.5 to 3 hours to fully benefit from the keynote, breakout sessions, and institution interactions.

Yes, once your registration is complete and your mobile number is verified via OTP, you will instantly receive your digital entry pass on WhatsApp.
