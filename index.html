Yes, general admission is free. However, given the premium nature and limited capacity of our venues, pre-registration is mandatory. Some specialized 1-on-1 consultations may have an optional premium booking fee.

Should parents attend with the student?

Absolutely. Career decisions are family decisions. We highly encourage parents to attend the mainstage sessions and participate in the guidance process.

How long will the entire experience take?

We recommend setting aside 2.5 to 3 hours to fully benefit from the keynote, breakout sessions, and institution interactions.
